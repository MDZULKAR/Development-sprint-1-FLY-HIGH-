Referencing

By citing and referencing the material consulted for a piece of academic work you not only enable others to verify facts ideas in your work more easily, but you will also be acting in an ethical and honest manner. Acknowledgement may be in the form of in text citations, footnotes, endnotes and/or a bibliography. Different citing and referencing styles cater for the specific requirements of different disciplines.

Why reference?

There are many reasons for you to use references. You are indicating that you have read published writing in the area you are studying. Referencing allows you support your arguments.
